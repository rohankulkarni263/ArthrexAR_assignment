To try this AR experience, please follow below steps:

1. Take a print-out of the given image target (Located in build folder).
2. Place it on the horizontal ground.
3. Take "Arthrex_AR_App1.apk" and Install it on Andriod mobile. (Android Naught and further devices).
4. Give all the required permissions to the app during installation.
5. After successfull installation, Camera of mobile device will start(I have tested this app on OnePlus 7 device).
6. Point the camera to the image target placed on ground.
7. Thats all, you can see the augmented Human Skeleton onto the image target.
